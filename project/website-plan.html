Individual Project Site Plan

Site Name:

Peru Experience

  • This name focuses on the main purpose of the site, which is to provide a memorable and comprehensive experience for tourists exploring Peru.

Site purpose:

For the Peru Experience

  • It is a common website that shows information about my country, some famous places, culture, food, and more. This website provides valuable information like the best season, the weather, local currency, and more tips.

Scenarios:

  • What are the top tourist attractions to visit in Peru?

    This question will help drive content related to popular landmarks, cultural spots, and must-see attractions in Peru, including Machu Picchu, Cusco, Lima, and more, with descriptions, images, and recommendations based on visitor preferences.

  • What are the best outdoor activities and natural wonders to enjoy in Peru?

    This scenario will guide content about Peru's incredible natural landscapes, including the Andes Mountains, Amazon Rainforest, Lake Titicaca, and Colca Canyon, catering to adventure seekers and nature enthusiasts.

Color Schema:

The color schema for this site includes two main colors:

  • Andean Sunrise (hsla(45, 100%, 45%, 1)): This vibrant orange-yellow color is used for headings and accents, inspired by the beautiful sunrises in the Andes mountains, giving the site a warm and inviting feel.
  • Inca White (hsla(30, 20%, 95%, 1)): This light, neutral color is used as the background color to provide a clean and pleasant visual experience, reminiscent of the traditional textiles and architecture of Peru.

Example CSS HSL:

    /* CSS HSL */
    --black: hsla(0, 0%, 0%, 1);
    --deep-purple: hsla(270, 30%, 20%, 1);
    --andean-sunrise: hsla(45, 100%, 45%, 1);
    --peru-orange: hsla(30, 80%, 55%, 1);
    --andes-blue: hsla(200, 60%, 50%, 1);
    --inca-white: hsla(30, 20%, 95%, 1);
    --white: hsla(0, 0%, 100%, 1);

Typography:

1. Primary Font: Oswald (sans-serif)

  • Usage: Oswald will be used for all headings across the site to give a bold, modern look and help guide users as they explore different sections.
  • Applied to:
    • Headings (h1, h2, h3, h4): All headings on the website will use Oswald to clearly define sections and make it easy for users to navigate.

2. Secondary Font: Source Sans Pro (sans-serif)

  • Usage: Source Sans Pro will be used for all body text and paragraphs, ensuring that the content is easy to read and visually appealing for users.
  • Applied to:
    • Body Text (p): All paragraphs, descriptions of tourist attractions, and general content on the site will use Source Sans Pro for readability.
    • Links and Buttons: Interactive elements like links and buttons will also use Source Sans Pro to maintain consistency in design.
